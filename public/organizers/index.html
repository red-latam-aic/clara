Organisers and contact

The conference “AI Latam Elevator Pitch” is organized by the Latinamerican Network of Artificial Intelligence in the Sciences (RELIAC) which is a network of latinoamerical scientists on different parts of the world that uses AI in their current research. You can find more information about RELIAC in the website.

This conference is independently organized by:

  • Dr. Juan Carlos San Vicente, Temple University, USA.
  • Dr. Manuel Szewc, Universidad de San Martin, Argentina.
  • Dr. Luis Itza Vazquez-Salazar, Heidelberg University, Germany.

You can contact us at: red.latam.aic@gmail.com
