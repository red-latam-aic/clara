Registration

Registration is free and open to everyone. You need to register to participate in the conference; you will receive the link and more information before the event. As part of the registration, you also have the option of submit an abstract for a 10 minute talk followed by 5 minutes of questions where you can show how you use AI in your research. There are a limited number of spots for talks, but not for participation in the conference.
