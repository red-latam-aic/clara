## About 

CLARA is the inaugural conference of the Latin American AI in Sciences network. It brings together researchers and students across the region who use machine learning and artificial intelligence to advance chemistry, physics, biology, and the sciences more broadly.

Our goal is to build a lasting community, share methods and results, and open paths for collaboration across Latin America and beyond. The conference is fully online and free to attend, so anyone can join from anywhere.
