# Conferencia LAtinoamericana de la Red de inteligencia Artificial en las ciencias.

## Welcome

Artificial intelligence (AI) is creating a revolution in society with increasingly common applications 
that are becoming more integrated into everyday life. This impact has also been growing in the exact sciences, 
where various applications of AI methodologies are gaining increasing importance.

On the other hand, the AI ​​revolution is still developing in Latin America. 
Considering this, our goal is to bring together leading representatives 
of the community to create a network where students, researchers, institutions, 
associations, and society as a whole can interact to develop joint projects,
 foster collaborations, and generally contribute to the development of AI in Latin America.
